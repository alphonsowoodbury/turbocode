# Context: Marketing Strategy & Positioning

## Product Positioning

### Tagline
**"Your thoughts, remembered."**

### Value Proposition
Context is the infinite canvas for your mind. No organizing, no formatting - just capture your thoughts and move on. The app remembers where you were, what you were listening to, and when it happened, transforming simple notes into rich, searchable memories. With Folios (Pro), your stream of consciousness becomes living intelligence.

## Target Audience

### Primary Market
- **Knowledge Workers** (25-45): Developers, designers, writers, consultants
- **Lifelong Learners**: Students, researchers, autodidacts
- **Mindful Professionals**: Those pursuing personal growth alongside career

### User Personas

**The Builder**
- Captures technical decisions, debugging notes, architecture ideas
- Needs: Fast capture during coding, automatic technical documentation
- Hook: "Your CLAUDE.md updates itself"

**The Reflector**
- Daily journaling, therapy prep, personal growth tracking
- Needs: Private space, pattern recognition, session preparation
- Hook: "Your therapist will thank you"

**The Creator**
- Story ideas, inspiration, creative fragments
- Needs: Capture without structure, serendipitous rediscovery
- Hook: "Never lose another shower thought"

- ✓ AI-powered synthesis

## Key Differentiators

### vs. Apple Notes
- **Automatic context capture** (location, music, time)
- **Timeline-first organization** (recency = relevance)
- **Folios** turn notes into living documents
- **Zero-friction entry** (one tap to capture)

### vs. Day One
- **No manual logging** - context captured automatically
- **Intelligence over archival** - active synthesis, not passive storage
- **Modern pricing** - generous free tier, single Pro tier
- **Developer-friendly** - auto-maintains technical docs

### vs. Notion
- **No setup required** - works instantly
- **Mobile-first** - built for iOS, not ported from web
- **Personal, not collaborative** - your private thinking space
- **Speed** - capture in seconds, not minutes

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Beta Community (Months 1-2)
- TestFlight with 1,000 power users
- Focus on developers and productivity enthusiasts
- Gather feedback on Folios concept
- Build word-of-mouth advocates

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Month 3)
- App Store release with free tier only
- Focus on habit formation
- Gather usage patterns for Folios
- Build email list for Pro launch

### Phase 3: Pro Launch (Month 4)
- Introduce Folios and Pro tier
- Launch week: 50% off annual
- Push notification to all users
- Press outreach with Folios story

### Phase 4: Growth (Months 5-12)
- Iterate based on user feedback
- Add platform features (widgets, shortcuts)
- Explore iPad and Mac versions
- Build sustainable MRR

## Marketing Channels
