## Core Philosophy: Zero-Friction, Maximum Value

### Zero-Friction Entry & Retrieval
- **Lightning-fast capture**: From thought to entry in under 3 seconds
- **Natural language processing**: Write however feels natural—Context understands
- **Intelligent type detection**: Automatically categorizes entries as Thoughts, Ideas, Tasks, Info, Questions, or Decisions
- **Voice-to-text integration**: Speak your thoughts directly into entries
- **Quick entry widgets**: Capture from anywhere on your device

### Privacy-First Design
- **Local-first architecture**: Your data lives on your device, not in the cloud
- **End-to-end encryption**: When sync arrives, your privacy remains absolute
- **No tracking, no ads**: Your thoughts are yours alone
- **Transparent data handling**: You control what gets processed and how

### Utility Over Engagement
- **No addiction mechanics**: Designed to serve you, not capture your attention
- **Meaningful insights**: AI that helps you understand patterns, not just data
- **Purposeful interactions**: Every feature serves your knowledge growth
- **Respectful notifications**: Only when truly valuable

### Apple-Grade User Experience
- **Native iOS/macOS design**: Feels like it belongs on your device
- **Fluid animations**: Every interaction is smooth and delightful
- **Accessibility-first**: Works beautifully for everyone
- **Dark mode excellence**: Stunning in any lighting condition
